In Order to Sign-Up for Testing

In order to get more information or to sign up for any of our tactile pattern-processing experiments, please e-mail us and include the following information

  • Your Full Name
  • Your E-Mail Address
  • Your University Phone Extension
  • Your Home Phone

    And indicate whether you would be interested in either short- or long-term experiments. Short-term studies might involve from 1 to 5 sessions within a week or two, while long-term studies could last for several months. In both cases, scheduling would be at your convenience. So please provide us with your interest in either (or both)

  • Short Experiment Duration
  • Long Experiment Duration

    Please email us with this information at pucclabs@princeton.edu
