Vibrotactile Devices in use at
The Tactile Research Laboratory at NAMRL

We have used some of the following commercially-available vibrotactile systems at the Tactile Research Laboratory at NAMRL:

  • Bruel and Kjaer 4810 Mini Shaker
  • Engineering Acoustics, Inc. C-2 Tactor (and EAI Tactor Driver systems)
  • PCB 303A Accelerometer (other similar versions currently available)
  • Pancake-style Pager Motors from Jameco, Hosfeldt, or MPJA
  • Tactaid tactors from Acoustical Engineering Corp.
  • Steadfast Technology R-1 Rototactor (apparently no longer available)
  • Steadfast Technology R-2  (apparently no longer available)
  • Steadfast Technology Pneumatic Components (apparently no longer available)
  • Steadfast Technology Tactile Guidance Systems (apparently no longer available)
